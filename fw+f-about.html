An Aussie now based in California, Trina found through photography a sense of adventure and freedom, and in photography a medium to tell stories.

For that reason, Trina was drawn to lifestyle photography, which she has kept up through her travels around the world and neighborhood meanderings.

The name Flat Whites + Fins is an ode to her Aussie roots - excellent coffee & surf. Contact Trina at trina.jp.ng@gmail.com for consultations about images for your home, purchases or simply to say hi.
